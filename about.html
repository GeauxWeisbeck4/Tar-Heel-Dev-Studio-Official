To Be, Rather Than to Seem

Many people don't know this, but the term Tar Heel is technically a derogatory term. The term comes from North Carolina's naval industry in the 1850. "Lowly, indolent, and poor" citizens were called Tar Heels because of the tar that stuck to to their heels while doing the hardest and worst jobs in the industry..


I don't view the term as in the least bit insulting. I have no problem being included with Carolinians that work so damn hard, they got pine tar stuck to the bottom of their feet. I can assure you that the commitment of Tar Heel Studio to its work and clients doesn't involve any dirty or stinky feet, but it will involve a relentless development process and continuous innovation. Clients are involved in the design process and I make sure that they understand each phase of the project from inception to deployment.

I may be the new kid on the block in the Triangle where the competition is stiff, but I guarantee that you will not find a better project development experience than you will at Tar Heel Dev Studio. Whether you design your brand's t-shirts or business website with us, you can take comfort in knowing that you hired somebody who enjoys serving you wholeheartedly and is truly invested in the success of your business. Trust me, I don't take it lightly that my business is named after the wonderful people of North Carolina. It's an honor to bear the name and serve those who choose to be, rather than seem.

Values and Ethics over Money

We Create Experiences, Not Products

When you think about your favorite memories or why you like your favorite business, it really never is anything about a physical product. Yes, you might love your Xbox - but you love it for the experience. That is how we approach design and development. Our entire focus is helping our clients create experiences for their customers, while we also make the development process a pleasant time spent with us. Customer-centered innovation and continuous solution deployment will build your business a large and loyal fanbase because people will feel good about your business. We love to build those experiences with you and consider experience in every phase of development.

Reducing Cost and Waste, While Improving Quality

We believe in sustainable solutions that make our products more affordable for customers, because we can create more, with less. Our process was developed this way to make the planet better for future generations, or at least to provide a model that sets that intention. But we never compromise quality for cost. All quotes are based on how we can best budget our resources to create the best possible web apps, designs, and marketing campaigns for your dollar. Don't just trust anyone for your project - trust a Tar Heel.
